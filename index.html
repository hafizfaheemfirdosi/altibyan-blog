About Al Tibyan Quran Academy

Al Tibyan Quran Academy is a premier Islamic educational institution dedicated to spreading the knowledge of the Holy Quran and authentic Islamic teachings through traditional and modern methods.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by Hafiz Muhammad Faheem, our academy began as a small local madrasah and has grown into an international online Quran teaching platform serving students from over 30 countries.

Our Mission

To provide quality Quranic education to Muslims worldwide, preserving the authentic recitation and understanding of the Quran as taught by our beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) through qualified scholars.

Our Methodology
  • One-on-one personalized attention
  • Traditional teaching with modern technology
  • Emphasis on understanding along with recitation
  • Balanced approach combining knowledge and spirituality
Our Teachers

All our instructors are certified Huffaz and Islamic scholars with Ijazah in Quran recitation and various Islamic sciences from recognized institutions like Al-Azhar University and Darul Uloom Deoband.

1. Enrollment

By enrolling in our courses, you agree to attend classes regularly and complete assigned homework.

Course fees are payable monthly in advance. We offer a 3-day grace period for payments.

Students must inform teachers at least 6 hours before missing a class. Repeated absences may result in termination.

4. Conduct

Students must maintain Islamic etiquette during classes. Disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

5. Refunds

Fees for completed months are non-refundable. Future months can be refunded if requested before the billing date.
